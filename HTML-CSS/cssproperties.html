  • text-align: left | right | center | justify | initial | inherit;
  • Note: font-weight: normal | bold | bolder | lighter | number | initial | inherit; It can also be a value between 100 | 200 | 300 | 400 | 500 | 600 | 700 | 800 | 900 where 100 is thin and 900 is extra bold
  • font-size
  • font-family
  • text-decoration: none: no line is drawn, and any existing decoration is removed. underline: draws a 1px line across the text at its baseline. line-through: draws a 1px line across the text at its “middle” point. overline: draws a 1px line across the text, directly above its “top” point. inherit: inherits the decoration of the parent.
  • color: Note: We generally only use black or white. Other colors such as red, blue, green are only good for testing purposes. You should use Hexadecimal values instead.
  • color: Use a color picker to find the color you need. Note: #000000 is black, #ffffff is white. #ffffff is the same as #fff, #FFFFFF or #fff or white.
  • background: You can also use an image as a background.
